BeeSweeper is a clone of the classic Minesweeper that until recently would come pre-installed on Windows. Your objective is to reveal every "free" square and avoid the beehives. Clues will show up (as variously coloured numbers) they indicate the amount of mines in adjacent squares. When you think you've found a mine right click to lay a warning flag, click the bee to restart the game.
